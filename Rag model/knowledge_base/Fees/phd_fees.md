## Admission Fee (PhD Programs)
- Admission Fee: ₹15,000 (Non-Refundable)
- Applicable at the time of admission for all PhD programs
- Paid once at the time of joining

## PhD Registration Fee
- One-Time Registration Fee: ₹10,000
- Paid at the time of initial enrollment
- Non-Refundable

## PhD Full Time — Annual Research Fee
- Annual Research Fee: ₹25,000 per year
- Applicable for all Full Time PhD scholars
- Departments: CSE, ECE, EEE, Mechanical, Civil, Management, Pharmacy, Agriculture, Sciences
- Duration: 3 to 5 years

## PhD Part Time — Annual Research Fee
- Annual Research Fee: ₹30,000 per year
- Applicable for all Part Time PhD scholars
- Departments: CSE, ECE, EEE, Mechanical, Civil, Management, Pharmacy, Agriculture, Sciences
- Duration: 4 to 6 years

## PhD Course Work Fee
- Course Work Fee: ₹15,000 (One Time)
- Paid in the first semester only
- Mandatory for all PhD scholars (Full Time and Part Time)
- Duration: 1 semester

## PhD Thesis Submission Fee
- Thesis Submission Fee: ₹10,000 (One Time)
- Paid at the time of final thesis submission
- Includes evaluation and examination charges

## PhD Re-Registration Fee (If Extended Beyond Stipulated Duration)
- Re-Registration Fee: ₹5,000 per semester
- Applicable if scholar exceeds the maximum duration
- Subject to approval by Research Committee

## PhD Fellowship and Fee Waiver
- UGC / CSIR / GATE / ICAR fellowship holders: Annual Research Fee waived
- Waiver subject to submission of valid fellowship sanction letter every year
- Fellowship scholars must maintain active fellowship status
- Applicable Departments: CSE, ECE, EEE, Mechanical, Management, Pharmacy, Agriculture

## PhD Total Estimated Cost — Full Time (3 Years)
- Admission Fee: ₹15,000 (one time)
- Registration Fee: ₹10,000 (one time)
- Course Work Fee: ₹15,000 (one time)
- Annual Research Fee: ₹25,000 x 3 years = ₹75,000
- Thesis Submission Fee: ₹10,000 (one time)
- Estimated Total (3 Years): ₹1,25,000

## PhD Total Estimated Cost — Part Time (4 Years)
- Admission Fee: ₹15,000 (one time)
- Registration Fee: ₹10,000 (one time)
- Course Work Fee: ₹15,000 (one time)
- Annual Research Fee: ₹30,000 x 4 years = ₹1,20,000
- Thesis Submission Fee: ₹10,000 (one time)
- Estimated Total (4 Years): ₹1,70,000

## PhD Hostel Fee (For Full Time Residential Scholars)
- Non-AC Room: ₹1,15,000 per year
- AC Room: ₹1,30,000 per year
- Includes: Laundry expenses and medical insurance up to ₹2,00,000
- Available for Full Time PhD scholars only

## PhD Fee Payment Schedule
- Admission Fee + Registration Fee: Paid at time of joining
- Course Work Fee: Paid in Semester 1
- Annual Research Fee: Paid at the beginning of each academic year
- Thesis Fee: Paid at time of thesis submission
- Payment Mode: Online / DD in favour of "Aditya University"
