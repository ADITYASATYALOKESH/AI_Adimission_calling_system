## Technical Hub — Overview
- Location: Aditya University Campus, Surampalem, Andhra Pradesh
- The Technical Hub is a dedicated innovation and skill-development center on campus designed to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry-ready skills.
- It serves as a central hub for coding, robotics, IoT, AI/ML, cybersecurity, and emerging technology training for all students.
- Open to all enrolled students across UG, PG, and PhD programs.

## Technical Hub — Facilities and Labs
- **Advanced Computing Lab**: High-performance workstations with GPU clusters for AI/ML and deep learning projects.
- **IoT and Embedded Systems Lab**: Equipped with Arduino, Raspberry Pi, ESP32 boards, sensors, and actuators for hands-on IoT prototyping.
- **Robotics and Automation Lab**: Robotic arms, autonomous vehicle kits, and simulation software for robotics programming.
- **Cybersecurity Lab**: Dedicated environment for ethical hacking, penetration testing, and network security training with industry-standard tools.
- **AR/VR Lab**: Augmented Reality and Virtual Reality development kits (Meta Quest, HoloLens) for immersive application development.
- **Cloud Computing Studio**: AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud sandbox environments for hands-on cloud architecture and DevOps training.
- **Data Science Workspace**: Tools including Python, R, Tableau, Power BI, and Jupyter Notebooks for analytics and visualization.
- **Maker Space**: 3D printers, laser cutters, and electronics fabrication tools for building hardware prototypes.

## Technical Hub — Programs and Certifications
- **Industry Certification Tracks**: Preparation and training for certifications including AWS Cloud Practitioner, Microsoft Azure Fundamentals, Google Data Analytics, Cisco CCNA, and CompTIA Security+.
- **Skill Development Workshops**: Regular weekend workshops on trending technologies — Generative AI, Large Language Models, Blockchain, DevOps, and Full Stack Development.
- **Bootcamps**: Intensive 2–4 week bootcamps during semester breaks covering Data Structures & Algorithms, Machine Learning, and Web Development.
- **Hackathons and Coding Contests**: Monthly internal hackathons and participation support for national-level competitions like Smart India Hackathon (SIH), HackWithInfy, and CodeChef contests.
- **Open Source Contribution Program**: Mentored program guiding students to contribute to GitHub open source projects and build public portfolios.

## Technical Hub — Industry Partnerships and Collaboration
- Collaborates with companies including TCS, Infosys, Wipro, IBM, Microsoft, and Google for curriculum-aligned skill programs.
- Industry mentors from partner companies conduct live project sessions and code reviews.
- Select students are sponsored for paid internships and pre-placement opportunities through Technical Hub performance.
- Annual Industry Connect Summit hosted at the hub — brings together recruiters, alumni, and students for networking and live project showcases.

## Technical Hub — Clubs and Student Communities
- **Coding Club (CodeCell)**: Weekly competitive programming sessions, team practice for ICPC and other contests.
- **AI/ML Club**: Research paper reading groups, model building challenges, and Kaggle competition teams.
- **Cybersecurity Club (CyberShield)**: Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions, vulnerability research, and awareness campaigns.
- **Robotics Club**: Design and build autonomous robots for inter-college and national robotics competitions.
- **Web Dev Society**: Full-stack project builds, UI/UX design sprints, and open source collaboration.
- All clubs are student-led with faculty advisors from relevant departments.

## Technical Hub — Innovation and Incubation Support
- Supports student startup ideas through the Aditya Innovation Cell (AIC).
- Selected student projects receive seed funding, mentorship, and workspace to develop into viable products.
- Connects student entrepreneurs with industry mentors, angel investors, and government startup schemes (Startup India, T-Hub).
- Past incubated startups span EdTech, HealthTech, AgriTech, and SaaS domains.

## Technical Hub — Access and Membership
- Access is free for all Aditya University students with a valid student ID.
- Extended lab hours: 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM (Monday to Saturday); 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM (Sunday).
- Students can book specialized lab slots (Robotics, AR/VR, Cybersecurity) via the campus portal.
- Project teams may apply for dedicated workspace allocation for semester-long projects.
