## PhD General Eligibility and Entry Requirements
- Minimum Qualification: Master's degree (M.Tech / M.E / M.Sc / MBA / M.Pharmacy) with minimum 55% marks
- Entrance: Aditya Research Entrance Test (ARET) + Personal Interview
- Selection: ARET Score (60%) + Interview Score (40%)
- Course Work: Mandatory 1 semester for all PhD scholars before research begins
- Modes Available: Full Time (3 to 5 years) and Part Time (4 to 6 years)

## PhD Admission Process — Step by Step
- Step 1: Apply online at research.adityauniversity.in
- Step 2: Appear for Aditya Research Entrance Test (ARET) — held twice a year (January and July)
- Step 3: Shortlisted candidates called for personal interview with the Research Committee
- Step 4: Final selection based on ARET score (60%) + Interview (40%)
- Step 5: Allotment of research supervisor based on area of interest
- Step 6: Complete mandatory course work in Semester 1
- Step 7: Begin full research after course work clearance

## PhD ARET Exam Schedule
- January Session Application: November 1 to December 15
- January Session Exam: Second week of January
- July Session Application: May 1 to June 15
- July Session Exam: Second week of July
- Results: Declared within 3 weeks of exam date
- Admission Completion: Within 2 weeks of result declaration
