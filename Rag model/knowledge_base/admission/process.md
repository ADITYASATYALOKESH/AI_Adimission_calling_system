## Admission Process Overview
- Aditya University offers admission through 3 routes: EAPCET / JEE / NEET ranks, ASAT (Aditya Scholastic Aptitude Test), and Direct Merit
- All admissions are processed through the official portal: admissions.adityauniversity.in
- Admission Fee: ₹15,000 (Non-Refundable) payable at time of joining
- Contact: Dr. A. Rama Krishna — Cell: 76590 76662 | Dr. R. Giri Prasad — Cell: 70950 76663

## Route 1 — Admission via EAPCET / JEE / NEET Rank

### Step 1: Appear for Entrance Exam
- AP EAPCET: Conducted by APSCHE for AP and Telangana students
- JEE Main: Conducted by NTA for students across India
- NEET: For B.Pharmacy and Pharma-D admissions

### Step 2: Apply to Aditya University
- Visit: admissions.adityauniversity.in
- Fill the online application form
- Upload documents: 10th marksheet, 12th marksheet, entrance rank card, Aadhaar, passport photo
- Application fee: Nil (Free application)

### Step 3: Receive Admission Call Letter
- Based on rank and seat availability, Aditya University sends a call letter via email and SMS
- Call letter includes: Reporting date, time, documents checklist, fee payment details

### Step 4: Report to Campus
- Report on the given date with all original documents
- Document verification by Admissions Committee
- Pay ₹15,000 Admission Fee (Non-Refundable) + first semester tuition fee
- Collect admission confirmation letter and student ID

### Step 5: Scholarship Processing
- Scholarship calculated at time of admission based on rank or ASAT score
- Scholarship amount adjusted in fee payment challan
- Submit scholarship documents (if applying for category or sports scholarship)

## Route 2 — Admission via ASAT (Aditya Scholastic Aptitude Test)

### Step 1: Register for ASAT
- Register at: admissions.adityauniversity.in
- ASAT Fee: ₹500 per attempt (Maximum 2 attempts)
- ASAT is conducted online — can be taken from home on your own device
- Test covers: Aptitude, Reasoning, English, Mathematics or Science (based on program)

### Step 2: Appear for ASAT
- Receive hall ticket on registered email 2 days before exam
- Take test on scheduled date and time
- Results declared within 48 hours of test

### Step 3: Receive Merit Scholarship Offer
- Based on ASAT score, scholarship offer sent via email
- Scholarship slabs: 10% to 75% of tuition fee based on score
- Accept the offer and confirm admission intent online

### Step 4: Report to Campus and Complete Admission
- Report on given date with original documents
- Document verification, fee payment, and admission confirmation
- Collect student ID and admission letter

## Route 3 — Direct Merit Admission (BBA, B.Sc, BCA, MBA, M.Sc)
- Applicable for programs where ASAT or government entrance is optional
- Apply online at admissions.adityauniversity.in
- Shortlisting based on 10+2 or graduation percentage
- Personal interview or counselling call by Admissions Team
- Report to campus on given date for document verification and fee payment

## Documents Required at Time of Admission (All Programs)
- 10th Class Marksheet and Certificate (original + 2 photocopies)
- 12th Class / Intermediate Marksheet and Certificate (original + 2 photocopies)
- Entrance Exam Rank Card — EAPCET / JEE / NEET / ASAT (original + 2 photocopies)
- Transfer Certificate (TC) from previous institution (original)
- Migration Certificate (for students from outside AP and TS)
- Conduct Certificate from previous institution
- Aadhaar Card (original + 2 photocopies)
- Passport size photographs — 6 copies
- Caste Certificate — if applicable (SC / ST / OBC / EWS)
- Income Certificate — if applying for category scholarship
- Medical Fitness Certificate from a registered doctor
- Anti-Ragging Affidavit — signed by student and parent (format available at admissions office)

## Lateral Entry B.Tech — Special Process
- Eligible: Diploma holders applying for direct II Year B.Tech admission
- Entrance: ECET 2026 (AP or TS)
- Apply at: admissions.adityauniversity.in (Lateral Entry section)
- Additional Document: Diploma marksheets (all semesters) and provisional certificate
- Scholarship up to 100% based on ECET 2026 rank (Rank 1-25 gets 100%)
