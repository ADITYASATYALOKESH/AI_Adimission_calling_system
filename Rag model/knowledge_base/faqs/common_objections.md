## Q: How can I apply for admission to Aditya University?
Applying for admission is a simple process. Visit apply.adityauniversity.in and enter your name (as per official records), your email ID, mobile number (preferably WhatsApp), program and specialization. Click on the validation link sent to your email so that you can be redirected to your dashboard. Fill the application by clicking Apply Now. Once the application is submitted, login credentials to your exclusive dashboard will be sent to your email ID. Our admission counsellors will guide you in case of any issues.

## Q: What are the UG Programs offered by Aditya University?
Aditya University offers UG Programs like B.Tech, BBA, B.Sc., BCA, and Pharmacy (B.Pharmacy and Pharma-D).

## Q: What are the PG Programs offered by Aditya University?
Aditya University offers a diverse range of PG Programs like M.Tech, MBA, M.Sc., MCA, and M.Pharmacy.

## Q: In which disciplines does Aditya University offer PhD?
Aditya University offers PhD in various disciplines like Engineering, Management, Humanities, Sciences, and Pharmaceutical Sciences.

## Q: Are there any industry collaborated programs offered in B.Tech?
Yes, Aditya University offers B.Tech CSE in association with SAP and Google Cloud, AIML in association with Microsoft and Google Cloud, and CSE Data Science in association with Google Cloud.

## Q: What are the eligibility criteria for admission into Aditya University?
For B.Tech and Pharmacy programs, candidates need to score a minimum of 60% or equivalent CGPA in Intermediate / Higher Secondary / XII Class from a recognised State or Central Board. For BBA, BCA, and B.Sc programs, the minimum requirement is 55% or equivalent CGPA.

## Q: What is the fee for B.Tech at Aditya University?
B.Tech tuition fees vary by branch. CSE is ₹2,75,000 per year, AI&ML is ₹2,50,000 per year, CSE Data Science and ECE are ₹2,00,000 per year, and EEE, Civil, Mechanical, Petroleum, Agricultural, and Mining Engineering are ₹1,00,000 per year. Admission Fee of ₹15,000 is additional and non-refundable. For complete fee details visit adityauniversity.in.

## Q: Does Aditya University offer scholarships?
Yes, Aditya University offers up to 100% scholarship for B.Tech, up to 75% scholarship for Pharmacy (B.Pharmacy and Pharma-D), and up to 20% scholarship for BBA, BCA, and B.Sc programs. Scholarships are based on entrance exam ranks (EAPCET, JEE, NEET, BIE, CBSE) or ASAT scores.

## Q: In how many instalments can I pay the tuition fee?
Tuition Fee, Hostel Fee, and Bus Fee can be paid in 2 instalments before the commencement of classwork every semester.

## Q: Is the admission fee included in the tuition fee?
No, the admission fee is not included in the tuition fee. The admission fee of ₹15,000 is paid only once in the first year (in II Year for lateral entry students) at the time of admission and is non-refundable.

## Q: Are there any sports scholarships available at Aditya University?
Yes, Aditya University offers 75% to 100% scholarships for candidates who have participated in National and International level sports meets. Subject to verification by the University constituted Sports Scholarship Committee.

## Q: Does Aditya University have a hostel on campus?
Yes, Aditya University has hostels inside the campus for both boys and girls.

## Q: Is there a provision for parents to stay on campus?
Yes, Aditya University hostels offer accommodation for guests including parents. The hostels are situated within the campus premises so that parents can enjoy all the facilities inside the campus.

## Q: Are the hostel rooms AC or Non-AC?
Aditya University has both AC and Non-AC hostel rooms on campus. Non-AC room fee is ₹1,15,000 per year and AC room fee is ₹1,30,000 per year. Both include laundry expenses and medical insurance up to ₹2,00,000.

## Q: What are the facilities available in the hostel?
Aditya University hostels offer separate hostels for boys and girls, well-furnished rooms, RO water in all student hostels, hot water facility, 24x7 ambulance and medical assistance, reading rooms, gym and library facility, North and South Indian cuisine, and beauty parlour among other facilities.

## Q: What entrance exams does Aditya University accept for B.Tech admission?
Aditya University accepts EAPCET (AP and Telangana), JEE Main, and ASAT (Aditya Scholastic Aptitude Test) for B.Tech admissions.

## Q: What is ASAT and how is it different from other entrance exams?
ASAT stands for Aditya Scholastic Aptitude Test. It is Aditya University's own entrance and scholarship test. It can be taken online from home or at a physical exam centre. Fee is ₹500 per attempt (maximum 2 attempts). Scholarships of 10% to 75% on tuition fee are offered based on ASAT scores. It is an alternative for students who do not have EAPCET or JEE scores.

## Q: Is there a lateral entry admission for diploma holders into B.Tech?
Yes, diploma holders can apply for direct admission into II Year (2nd Year) B.Tech through ECET 2026. Tuition fee for Lateral Entry is ₹1,00,000 per year. Scholarships up to 100% are available based on ECET 2026 rank — Rank 1 to 25 gets 100% scholarship, Rank 26 to 50 gets 75%, Rank 51 to 100 gets 50%, and Rank 101 to 150 gets 25%.

## Q: What accreditations does Aditya University hold?
Aditya University is accredited with NAAC A++ Grade (highest grade), NBA Tier 1 accreditation, NIRF Rank Band 151-200, Times Higher Education Impact Rank (Diamond Category), and QS I-Gauge Diamond Rating.

## Q: Is the scholarship applicable every year or only for the first year?
Scholarship is applicable for the first year only. To continue receiving the scholarship in upcoming years, students must maintain a minimum CGPA of 8.0 with no backlogs in every semester.
