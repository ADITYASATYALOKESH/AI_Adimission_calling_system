## Category Scholarship Overview
- Aditya University provides category-based scholarships to support students from reserved and special categories
- Designed to make quality education accessible to SC, ST, OBC, EWS, Differently Abled, and other special categories
- Applicable in addition to or separate from government scholarships
- All category scholarships subject to verification of valid certificates

## SC (Scheduled Caste) Scholarship
- Eligibility: Students belonging to SC category with valid caste certificate issued by competent authority (AP or TS)
- Benefit: Fee Reimbursement as per AP / TS Government norms
- Additional University Support: 10% additional discount on tuition fee for SC students not covered under government reimbursement
- Income Limit: As per AP / TS Government scholarship norms
- Documents Required:
  - Caste Certificate (issued by MRO or above)
  - Income Certificate (issued by MRO or above)
  - Aadhaar Card
  - Bank account details (student's own account)
  - Previous academic marksheets

## ST (Scheduled Tribe) Scholarship
- Eligibility: Students belonging to ST category with valid tribe certificate issued by competent authority (AP or TS)
- Benefit: Fee Reimbursement as per AP / TS Government norms
- Additional University Support: 10% additional discount on tuition fee for ST students not covered under government reimbursement
- Income Limit: As per AP / TS Government scholarship norms
- Documents Required:
  - Tribe Certificate (issued by MRO or above)
  - Income Certificate (issued by MRO or above)
  - Aadhaar Card
  - Bank account details (student's own account)
  - Previous academic marksheets

## OBC (Other Backward Class) Scholarship
- Eligibility: Students belonging to BC-A, BC-B, BC-C, BC-D, BC-E categories with valid caste certificate
- Benefit: Fee Reimbursement as per AP / TS Government norms (for eligible income group)
- Additional University Support: 5% additional discount on tuition fee for OBC students
- Income Limit: As per AP / TS Government scholarship norms
- Documents Required:
  - BC Caste Certificate (issued by MRO or above)
  - Income Certificate (issued by MRO or above)
  - Aadhaar Card
  - Bank account details
  - Previous academic marksheets

## EWS (Economically Weaker Section) Scholarship
- Eligibility: Students from General category with annual family income below ₹8,00,000
- Benefit: 10% concession on tuition fee
- Documents Required:
  - EWS Certificate issued by competent authority (Tehsildar / MRO)
  - Income Certificate
  - Aadhaar Card
  - Previous academic marksheets
- Note: EWS scholarship is applicable only to students not availing any other government scholarship

## Differently Abled (Divyangjan) Scholarship
- Eligibility: Students with minimum 40% disability as certified by Government Medical Board
- Benefit: 25% concession on tuition fee
- Additional Support: Priority seating in classrooms, accessible infrastructure, hostel ground floor allocation
- Documents Required:
  - Disability Certificate issued by Government Hospital / Medical Board
  - Aadhaar Card
  - Passport size photograph
  - Previous academic marksheets

## Single Girl Child Scholarship
- Eligibility: Student who is the only girl child in the family
- Benefit: 10% concession on tuition fee
- Applicable Programs: All UG and PG programs
- Documents Required:
  - Affidavit from parent confirming single girl child status
  - Aadhaar Card of student and parents
  - Family photo

## Defence and Ex-Servicemen Ward Scholarship
- Eligibility: Children of serving or retired Defence personnel (Army, Navy, Air Force)
- Benefit: 15% concession on tuition fee
- Documents Required:
  - Service Certificate or Discharge Certificate of parent
  - Dependent Certificate
  - Aadhaar Card of student and parent

## Aditya Alumni Ward Scholarship
- Eligibility: Children or siblings of Aditya University alumni
- Benefit: 10% concession on tuition fee
- Documents Required:
  - Proof of alumni relationship (alumni ID or degree certificate of parent or sibling)
  - Aadhaar Card

## NRI and Foreign National Category
- Eligibility: Students with NRI status or foreign nationality
- Tuition Fee: As per NRI fee structure (contact admissions office for current NRI fee)
- Scholarship: Merit-based NRI scholarship available for top performers
- Contact: Admissions Office for detailed NRI fee and scholarship structure

## Category Scholarship — Combination Rules
- Government scholarships (SC/ST/OBC/EWS reimbursement) can be combined with University merit scholarships
- University category concessions (Differently Abled, Single Girl Child, Defence, Alumni Ward) cannot be combined with each other
- Only the highest applicable University concession will be applied
- Government reimbursement scholarships are applied separately by the government directly to student accounts
- Sports scholarship cannot be combined with category scholarship — higher value is applied

## Category Scholarship Application Process
- Step 1: Collect Category Scholarship Application Form from Admissions Office or download from admissions.adityauniversity.in
- Step 2: Attach all required documents (self-attested copies)
- Step 3: Submit to the Scholarship Cell at Admissions Office before the deadline
- Step 4: Documents verified by the Scholarship Committee
- Step 5: Approved scholarships adjusted in fee payment challan
- Deadline: Must be submitted within 30 days of joining

## Government Scholarship Schemes Accepted
- AP Jagananna Vidya Deevena Scheme (SC, ST, BC, EWS, Minority)
- AP Jagananna Vasathi Deevena Scheme (Hostel support)
- TS Government Fee Reimbursement Scheme
- National Scholarship Portal (NSP) schemes
- Post Matric Scholarship by Ministry of Social Justice
- Students must apply independently on government portals — University provides required documents and support
