## Sports Scholarship Overview
- Aditya University offers merit-based sports scholarships to encourage students with outstanding sports achievements
- Applicable for aspirants with National and International level sports participation
- Subject to successful scrutiny by the University constituted Sports Scholarship Committee
- Scholarship is applicable for the first year only
- Renewal Condition: Maintain 8.0 CGPA with no backlogs in every semester

## Sports Scholarship Slabs
- 75% Scholarship: National level sports participation
- 100% Scholarship: International level sports participation
- No partial slabs — only National (75%) and International (100%) levels are considered

## Documents Required for Sports Scholarship
- Participation Certificate from State / National / International Sports Body
- Medal or Achievement Certificate (if applicable)
- Certificate must be from a recognized sports federation or Sports Authority of India (SAI)
- Certificate issued by School / College sports events are NOT eligible
- Aadhaar Card copy
- Recent passport size photograph
- Filled Sports Scholarship Application Form (available at admissions office)

## Sports Scholarship Verification Process
- Step 1: Submit application form along with all required documents at the Admissions Office
- Step 2: Documents verified by the University constituted Sports Scholarship Committee
- Step 3: Physical verification or trial may be conducted by the Sports Department
- Step 4: Committee decision is final and binding
- Step 5: Scholarship amount adjusted in the fee payment at time of admission
- Processing Time: 5 to 7 working days after document submission

## Sports Scholarship Terms and Conditions
- Scholarship is applicable for first year tuition fee only
- To continue scholarship in 2nd year and beyond: maintain 8.0 CGPA with no backlogs in every semester
- Sports scholarship cannot be combined with other merit scholarships (ASAT / JEE / EAPCET etc.)
- Higher of the two scholarships will be applied — student cannot claim both
- Scholarship is non-transferable
- Misrepresentation of documents leads to immediate cancellation of scholarship and disciplinary action
- Students must participate in University sports events and represent Aditya University when called upon

## Sports Scholarship — Renewal Conditions Year Wise
- 1st Year: Based on National or International participation certificate
- 2nd Year onwards: Minimum 8.0 CGPA + No backlogs in previous semesters + Continued sports participation
- Annual review by Sports Scholarship Committee
