## Scholarship General Rules
- Scholarship is applicable for the first year only
- To continue the scholarship in upcoming years: minimum 8.0 CGPA with no backlogs in every semester is mandatory
- ASAT Fee: ₹500 per attempt (Maximum 2 attempts allowed)
- For CBSE/+2 marks: English, Maths, Biology, Physics and Chemistry must be included while calculating percentage
- Minimum eligibility for B.Tech Programs: 60% or equivalent
- Minimum eligibility for BBA and B.Sc Programs: 55% or equivalent
