# Hostel Safety — Aditya University

## Safety Overview
- Aditya University maintains a zero-tolerance policy for ragging, harassment, and misconduct
- All hostels are equipped with round-the-clock security and surveillance
- Anti-Ragging Committee and Internal Complaints Committee are active on campus
- Campus is a safe residential environment for students from across India

## Security Infrastructure
- 24-hour security guards posted at all hostel gates (Boys and Girls hostels)
- CCTV cameras installed at: Entry gates, exit gates, corridors, stairways, and all common areas
- All footage monitored and recorded with 30-day retention
- Biometric attendance at hostel entry — records every entry and exit
- No unauthorised person permitted inside hostel premises at any time

## Anti-Ragging Policy
- Aditya University strictly follows UGC Anti-Ragging Regulations 2009
- Zero tolerance — any form of ragging leads to immediate expulsion
- Anti-Ragging Squad conducts surprise checks in hostels and campus
- Every student and parent must sign an Anti-Ragging Affidavit at time of admission
- Anti-Ragging Helpline: 1800-180-5522 (National Helpline — Toll Free)
- University Anti-Ragging Committee: Contact Hostel Warden or Dean of Students

## Girls Hostel Safety — Special Provisions
- Dedicated female security staff at Girls Hostel gate
- Female warden resident inside Girls Hostel at all times
- Gate closing time strictly enforced: 9:00 PM
- Male visitors not permitted inside Girls Hostel building under any circumstance
- Visitor meetings only at designated visitor waiting area
- Emergency panic buttons installed in Girls Hostel corridors and washrooms
- Internal Complaints Committee (ICC) for any harassment complaints

## Fire Safety
- Fire extinguishers installed on every floor of all hostels
- Smoke detectors in all rooms and corridors
- Fire exit routes clearly marked and kept unobstructed at all times
- Annual fire safety drill conducted for all hostel students
- Emergency evacuation plan displayed on every floor

## Medical Emergency Protocol
- On-campus medical centre with resident doctor: 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM
- 24-hour nurse stationed in hostel for after-hours emergencies
- First-aid kits available at every hostel floor and warden room
- Ambulance available on campus for immediate hospital transfer
- Emergency hospital tie-ups: Government Hospital Samalkot, Kakinada Government Hospital, Ramesh Hospitals Kakinada
- Medical insurance up to ₹2,00,000 included in hostel fee covers hospitalisation

## Visitor Policy
- Visitors allowed only in designated visitor rooms and waiting areas
- Visiting hours: 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM on all days
- Visitors must register at the security gate with a valid photo ID
- Parents and guardians must register their contact details at the time of student admission
- Overnight stay of visitors is strictly not permitted on hostel premises

## Student Grievance Redressal
- Any safety concern or complaint can be submitted to:
  - Hostel Warden (first point of contact)
  - Dean of Students
  - University Grievance Cell: grievance@adityauniversity.in
  - Internal Complaints Committee (for sexual harassment complaints)
- All complaints treated with strict confidentiality
- Resolution timeline: Acknowledgement within 24 hours, resolution within 7 working days
