## Hostel Overview
- Total Hostels: 4 (2 Boys Hostels + 2 Girls Hostels)
- Total Capacity: 2,400 students
- Location: Within campus — Surampalem, East Godavari, Andhra Pradesh
- Type: Separate hostels for Boys and Girls
- Management: Managed by University Hostel Administration

## Hostel Fee Structure
- Non-AC Room: ₹1,15,000 per year
- AC Room: ₹1,30,000 per year
- Includes: Laundry expenses and medical insurance up to ₹2,00,000
- Admission Fee: ₹15,000 (one time, non-refundable) separate from hostel fee
- Payment: Semester-wise payment accepted

## Room Types and Occupancy
- Double Sharing Room (Non-AC): Standard option for most students
- Double Sharing Room (AC): Premium option with air conditioning
- Triple Sharing Room (Non-AC): Economy option available on request
- Single Room: Available for PG and PhD scholars subject to availability
- All rooms include: Cot, mattress, study table, chair, wardrobe, and fan or AC

## Mess and Dining Facilities
- Separate mess for Boys and Girls hostels
- Vegetarian and Non-Vegetarian options available daily
- Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch, Evening Snacks, and Dinner
- Mess Timing:
  - Breakfast: 7:00 AM to 9:00 AM
  - Lunch: 12:00 PM to 2:00 PM
  - Evening Snacks: 4:30 PM to 5:30 PM
  - Dinner: 7:30 PM to 8:30 PM
- Special meals on festivals and university events
- Hygienic kitchen with RO purified water throughout the hostel

## Sports and Recreation Facilities
- Indoor Games Room: Carrom, Chess, Table Tennis, Foosball
- Outdoor Sports: Cricket ground, Football ground, Basketball court, Volleyball court
- Badminton courts within campus
- Gymnasium: Fully equipped gym available for hostel students
- Recreation Room: Television lounge with DTH connection

## Study and Academic Support
- 24-hour reading room and study hall
- High-speed Wi-Fi internet throughout hostel premises
- Computer lab access for hostel students during off-hours
- Separate quiet zones for exam preparation
- Power backup (UPS and generator) for uninterrupted study

## Safety and Security
- 24-hour security at all hostel gates
- CCTV surveillance at entry, exit, corridors, and common areas
- Biometric attendance system at hostel entry
- Visitor policy: Visitors allowed only in designated visitor rooms during visiting hours
- Visiting Hours: 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM on weekdays and weekends
- Emergency contact numbers displayed at all floors

## Medical Facilities
- On-campus medical centre with resident doctor
- 24-hour nurse available at hostel
- First-aid kit at every floor
- Medical insurance up to ₹2,00,000 included in hostel fee
- Tie-up with nearby hospitals in Samalkot and Kakinada for emergencies
- Ambulance facility available on campus

## Other Amenities
- RO water purifiers on every floor
- Washing machines in laundry room (laundry included in fee)
- Ironing facility available
- ATM on campus
- Stationery and grocery shop within campus
- Photocopy and printing facility
- Prayer room and meditation hall
- Courier and parcel collection point

## Hostel Rules and Regulations
- Hostel gate closing time: 9:00 PM (Students must return by 9:00 PM)
- Permission required for overnight absence — written request to Hostel Warden
- No ragging — zero tolerance policy strictly enforced
- No alcohol, tobacco, or illegal substances permitted
- Damage to hostel property will be charged to the student
- Students must maintain discipline and cleanliness in rooms and common areas

## Hostel Admission Process
- Step 1: Apply for hostel at the time of college admission
- Step 2: Hostel allotment based on availability and program
- Step 3: Pay hostel fee at the time of joining
- Step 4: Collect room keys and hostel ID from Hostel Office
- Preference given to: outstation students, first-year students, girl students
