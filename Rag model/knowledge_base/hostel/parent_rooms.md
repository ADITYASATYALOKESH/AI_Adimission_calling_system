# Parent Rooms — Aditya University

## Parent Room Overview
- Aditya University provides dedicated guest rooms for parents and guardians visiting their ward
- Located within the campus at Surampalem, East Godavari, Andhra Pradesh
- Designed to give parents a comfortable stay while visiting for admissions, parent-teacher meetings, or personal visits
- Prior booking is mandatory — walk-in availability not guaranteed

## Room Types and Charges
- Standard Room (Non-AC): ₹500 per night
  - Includes: 1 double bed, attached bathroom, fan, TV
- Standard Room (AC): ₹800 per night
  - Includes: 1 double bed, attached bathroom, AC, TV, hot water
- Family Suite (AC): ₹1,200 per night
  - Includes: 2 beds, sitting area, attached bathroom, AC, TV, hot water
  - Suitable for families with 3 to 4 members
- Checkout Time: 11:00 AM
- Check-in Time: 12:00 PM

## Facilities in Parent Guest Rooms
- Clean and well-maintained rooms with daily housekeeping
- Attached bathrooms with hot and cold water
- Television with DTH connection
- Wi-Fi internet access
- Bed linen and towels provided
- Power backup — uninterrupted electricity through UPS and generator
- Room service available between 7:00 AM and 10:00 PM

## Dining Arrangements for Parents
- Parents can avail meals at the University Guest Dining Hall
- Meal Charges (separate from room charges):
  - Breakfast: ₹80 per person
  - Lunch: ₹150 per person
  - Dinner: ₹150 per person
- Vegetarian and Non-Vegetarian options available
- Meals must be pre-ordered by informing the front desk one hour in advance
- Tea and coffee available at canteen from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM

## Booking Process
- Step 1: Contact the Admissions Office or Hostel Office at least 3 days in advance
- Step 2: Provide student name, roll number, program, and dates of visit
- Step 3: Room allotment confirmed via phone or email
- Step 4: Pay room charges at the Hostel Office on arrival
- Online booking: Available at admissions.adityauniversity.in (Guest Room Booking section)
- Payment Modes: Cash, UPI, Net Banking, Debit / Credit Card

## Availability and Booking Priority
- Priority 1: Parents visiting during admission season (June to August)
- Priority 2: Parents attending Parent-Teacher Meetings and Annual Day
- Priority 3: General visits throughout the year
- Peak season (admission time): Advance booking of 7 days strongly recommended
- Maximum stay: 7 consecutive nights per visit

## Important Dates When Parent Rooms Are in High Demand
- Admission Season: June to August every year
- Parent-Teacher Meeting: Conducted once per semester (dates communicated to students)
- Annual Day and Convocation: Typically in February or March
- Freshers Orientation: First week of July every year
- Book early during these periods to avoid unavailability

## Rules for Parent Guest Rooms
- Only parents and immediate family members (spouse, siblings) permitted to stay
- Guests must submit a valid photo ID (Aadhaar Card, Passport, Driving Licence) at check-in
- Alcohol and smoking strictly prohibited inside guest room premises
- Noise must be kept to a minimum after 10:00 PM
- Damage to room property will be charged to the guest
- Outsiders not permitted to enter hostel or student residential areas
- Parents can meet their ward only in the designated visitor meeting area
